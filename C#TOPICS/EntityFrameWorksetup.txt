





Dotnet migration commands-

*for adding migrations--
1.dotnet ef migrations add Add'ClassName'Table --context 'xxxxxx' --project '-------'
*for updating in database--
2.dotnet ef database update --project 'xxxxxx' --context '----------'

*for modifying a previously migrated table--
dotnet ef migrations add Added'newcolumnname'InTheTableTable -context ..... --project ....
